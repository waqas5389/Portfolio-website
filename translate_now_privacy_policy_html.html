TranslateNow — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Add Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how TranslateNow ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the TranslateNow mobile application. TranslateNow is developed by Muhammad Waqas on behalf of CodeMate.

1. No Collection of Personal Data

TranslateNow does not collect, store, or share any personal data. You do not need to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.

Specifically, TranslateNow does not collect:

  • Names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Usage analytics that identify you personally

2. Translation Data (Text & Voice Only)

To provide translations, TranslateNow sends the text and voice input you provide directly to the Google Translate API. The app does not support image uploads or image-based translation — no image data is sent or processed.

Important details:

  • The app does not store your text or voice content on our servers.
  • TranslateNow does not retain translation requests after the translation is completed.
  • We do not share your input with third parties except for the Google Translate API which processes the translation request.

3. Third-Party Services

TranslateNow uses the Google Translate API to perform translations. When you submit text or voice for translation, that data is transmitted to Google for processing. We do not control Google's processing or retention policies. Please review Google's privacy documentation for details about how Google handles data.

4. Data Security

Because TranslateNow does not store personal data, there is minimal risk of personal data exposure. All communication with the Google Translate API is performed over secure, encrypted channels (HTTPS).

5. Children

TranslateNow is not designed to collect information from children. Since we do not collect personal information, the app is appropriate for general audiences. If a parent believes their child has provided personal information inadvertently, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Thank you for using TranslateNow. We respect your privacy and aim to keep translations simple, fast, and secure.
