Privacy Policy
Last Updated: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Tranzola ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by Muhammad Waqas and operated under NayaPixel Innovation, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy rights.

Important: By downloading, installing, or using Tranzola, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app immediately.

1. Information We Collect

Tranzola collects and processes the following types of information:

1.1 Translation Data

When you use translation features, the text or voice input you provide is transmitted to the Google Translate API for processing. This data is:

  • Sent over secure HTTPS connections
  • Not stored on our servers
  • Processed according to Google's privacy policies
  • Deleted after translation completion

1.2 Payment Information

For in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details. Google processes all transactions securely.

1.3 Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports
  • Language preferences
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

1.5 Push Notification Tokens

Firebase Cloud Messaging stores device tokens to enable push notifications. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing translation services through Google Translate API
  • Processing in-app purchases and managing subscriptions
  • Displaying personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • Sending push notifications about app updates or features
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing bugs
  • Complying with legal obligations

No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data shared with service providers is only for the purposes described in this policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Translation data: Not retained; processed and immediately discarded
  • Advertising IDs: Retained by AdMob per their policies
  • Crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging
  • Consent preferences: Stored locally on your device

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission uses encrypted HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • No sensitive personal data is stored locally on your device
  • Third-party services comply with industry security standards
  • Regular security audits of our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Tranzola does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at waqas927212@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

7.1 European Union (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Opt-out of data processing for certain purposes
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke consent at any time

7.2 California, USA (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or shared
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7.3 Other Regions

Users in other jurisdictions may have similar rights under local privacy laws. Contact us to learn more about your specific rights.

To exercise your rights: Email us at waqas927212@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.
