1. Introduction

Welcome to Wallora. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while browsing our wallpaper collection. This policy explains how we handle your data and the specific permissions required to run the app.

2. Content Disclaimer (AI Generated)

Please note that the wallpapers displayed in Wallora are generated using Artificial Intelligence (AI). These images are digital creations designed for artistic and aesthetic purposes.

While we strive for high quality, strictly no real-world private individuals are depicted. If you find any content inappropriate, please report it to us via email.

3. Information Collection

We strictly do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number. You can use Wallora without creating an account.

4. App Permissions

To provide a smooth experience for viewing and downloading wallpapers, Wallora requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET Used to connect to our cloud database to fetch and display the latest wallpapers.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Used to verify your internet connection before loading high-quality images to save data and prevent errors.
  • SET_WALLPAPER The core feature of the app. It allows you to set any image as your Home Screen or Lock Screen wallpaper instantly.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Required only for Android 9 and older)
    Used to save wallpapers to your phone's gallery so you can access them offline.
